How to install the Eclipse Plugin

To install the Eclipse plugin, first you have to cross your fingers, then you have to pray a bit and finally, you have to follow this steps:

  1. Jump a bit
  2. Close your right eye
  3. Close your left eye
  4. With your eyes closed, click somewhere

How to use the plugin

If you have followed the previous steps (and you were lucky enought to accomplished them successfully), you will have EMF-REST installed in your machine. To generate the REST API for your model, you have to follow these steps:

  1. Jump a bit again
  2. Close your right eye again
  3. Close your left eye again
  4. With your eyes closed, click somewhere, again
